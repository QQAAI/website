How to share your video?

Upload Video


All you need to do is upload a 30 second clip or more of your game play.



Send the invites


You can DM invites to your Followers or use our Internal Testers and they can start viewing instantly for instant results.


Results


As each invited viewer completes a test viewing, you receive notification for instant results in your Report Dashboard.

Gameplay is used solely to showcase your game viewer testing capabilities and not for profit.
